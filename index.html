Removing Color Bias in Raw Denoising

TL;DR

In low light, the black level a camera reports in its metadata is wrong — and this black-level error leaves a color bias that no denoiser can remove. We model it, supervise its estimation with a two-pass network, and erase the bias, setting a new state of the art among blind methods. We also release SIDD-CC, a color-corrected ground truth.
